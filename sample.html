Budapest is the capital city of Hungary. It is best known for its arts and culture. It is a relatively small city, however there are much to see and do. ... Situated on thermal springs, there are many naturally heated baths to relax in, the Széchenyi baths are the largest with 15 indoor baths and 3 outdoor. There are many spectacular viewpoints in Budapest, great for capturing the views of the city. From 360 panoramic views up at St Stephens Basilica to a wide view of the parliament and th e River at Fisherman’s Bastion. Visit the Museum of Fine Arts and enjoy a day amongst famous European art. Classical music lovers will appreciate a performance at the Academy of Music.
